Alice Broughton

Owner, Draper, and Designer

Alice is a costume technician with 15 years of experience working in live theater and film. She holds an A.S. in Costume Construction Technology, a B.A. in History, and has continued with independent study of draping and couture sewing techniques. She specializes in historical menswear and womenswear, formal wear, and fantasy costuming.
